TIPS FOR YOUR GROUP

For Your Young Children (Ages 2-6):

  • Magic Kingdom is BEST for this age group - Frozen show, Minnie Mouse meets, gentle rides
  • Bring or rent strollers - parks are huge
  • Plan for afternoon naps or pool time at resort
  • Character dining experiences are worth it for this age
  • Skip intense rides - your kids are sensitive

For Your 6-Year-Old Nephew:

  • LEGOLAND is perfect for Lego lovers his age!
  • Star Wars at Disney's Hollywood Studios (Galaxy's Edge)
  • Grinchmas at Universal is age-appropriate
  • Harry Potter atmosphere (though main rides too intense)

For Your Parents (Ages 74-76):

  • All parks offer wheelchair/scooter rentals
  • Many shows and parades to enjoy while sitting
  • Disney Springs shopping with no admission required
  • Christmas lights viewing at Celebration or Lake Eola (easy walking)

For the Newborn (5 months):

  • Baby Care Centers at all Disney parks (nursing, changing, warming bottles)
  • Stroller as home base
  • Quieter activities like Disney Springs or Celebration

Multi-Generational Tips:

  • Split up! Not everyone needs to do everything together
  • Plan one "all together" activity per day (Christmas dinner, evening lights)
  • Use rider swap at parks so adults can take turns
  • Book 2 park days to spread out the experience
  • Rest days at resort pool between park days

SUGGESTED ITINERARY

Sunday Dec 21: Arrival, settle in, dinner, maybe visit Celebration "Now Snowing" (quick, close, magical)
Monday Dec 22: Magic Kingdom - Best for young kids (Frozen show, character meets, Jingle Cruise)
Tuesday Dec 23: EPCOT - Festival of the Holidays, Candlelight Processional, Frozen Ever After
Wednesday Dec 24 (Christmas Eve):
  • Morning: Disney Springs (FREE, shopping, lights, Santa) OR Resort pool day
  • Afternoon: Rest
  • Evening: Catholic Christmas Eve Mass
Thursday Dec 25 (Christmas Day):
  • Morning: Open presents at resort, relaxed morning
  • Afternoon: Christmas Day Restaurant Dinner (reservation required)
  • Evening: Drive to see Christmas lights (Lake Eola or Celebration)
Friday Dec 26:
  • Option A: Universal Studios/Islands of Adventure (Grinchmas, Harry Potter for adults/older kids)
  • Option B: LEGOLAND (perfect for 6-year-old nephew, Duplo area for little ones)
  • Option C: Resort pool + WonderWorks or Crayola Experience
Saturday Dec 27: Departure OR morning at Disney Springs shopping
